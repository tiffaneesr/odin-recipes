Chicken Quesadilla

Description

The secret to these super delicious, easy-as-pie weeknight quesadilla? A quick sauce made from flour and stock, which flavors the cooked veggies and helps them to stick together, so they don't fall out as you're eating.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. pluse 1 tsp. olive oil, divided
  • 4 tsp. all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp. chili powder
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded cooked chicken breasts
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • any extra garnishments you prefer

Directions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and chili powder. Cook, stirring, 1 to 2 minutes. Gradually whisk in stock. Add bell pepper and chicken. Cook, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender, 6 to 8 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in cheese.
  2. Heat 1/2 teaspoon oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Spread half of vegetable mixture on a tortilla, leaving a 1-inch border all around. Top with second tortilla. Cook, turning once, until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Repeat with remaining oil, tortillas, and vegetable mixture.
  3. Slice into wedges and serve topped with desired garnishes.
