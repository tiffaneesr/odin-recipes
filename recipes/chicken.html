Million Dollar Chicken

With bold and fresh flavors, this dish will be a hit with the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly cut or pounded
  • 6 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup crispy bacon crumbled
  • 1/2 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1 1/2 cup Colby Jack cheese, shredded

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix together cream cheese, mayo, bacon, green onion, and half of the cheddar cheese together in a medium-sized bowl.
  3. In a baking dish, evenly place the chicken breast and spreadthe cheese mixture over the chicken. Sprinkle the rest of the chedar cheese on top.
  4. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until the chicken has an internal reading of 165 degrees F.
  5. Top with a bacon and green onion garnish and serve!
